Visual Language :

Clean & Approachable - we use generous whitespace but not sterile emptiness. The UI breathes without feel. Not too rounded to feel childish, not too square to feel harsh.

Typography that guides - Headers are clear and readable (semibold, proper size). Section labels are subtle but visible. We dropped the aggressive uppercase tracking in favor of natural, readable text that doesn't scream at users.

Subtle depth through shadows - Soft box shadows (`shadow-sm`, `shadow-md`) give elements a gentle lift. Color inputs and controls have a slight tint background (`bg-muted/40`) to feel touchable without being heavy.

Interactive feedback - Hover states are smooth (`transition-all`). Buttons get a slight background shift. Selected items scale slightly and get shadows. The UI responds to touch without being distracting.


Visual hierarchy through grouping - Sections are clearly separated but flow naturally. Controls are grouped logically. Input fields have consistent heights and padding.

Presets that invite clicking - Gradient swatches are rounded squares that scale on hover. Selected state is obvious with rings and scale transforms.

Color palette - Warm cream background in light mode. Pure white cards. Near-black text. Subtle borders that define without imprisoning. The accent color is used sparingly as a punctuation mark.
